# Icon Models - New Vehicle Model Logic

Source: `New_Cart_Converter.php`

All Icon models share the manufacturer defaults in `../Manufacturer-Logic/Icon.md`.
Below are the model-specific differences.

Note: Icon has two distinct product lines:
- **C-Series** (Commercial): Brown seats, mostly White only, many utility beds, NOT street legal
- **i-Series** (Consumer): 2 Tone seats, full color palette, street legal
- **G-Series** (Gas): GAS powered (not electric), Black seats, limited colors
